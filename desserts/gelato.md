# Gelato recipe


## Preparation

- Chill the mixture thoroughly before churning
- Prepare an ice bath if needed
- Ensure your ice cream maker is ready (pre-frozen bowl if required)


## Ingredients

- 2 cups whole milk
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 0.5 cup sugar
- 4 egg yolks
- 1 tsp vanilla extract (or other flavoring)
- Pinch of salt


## Instructions

- Heat milk and cream in a saucepan until warm (do not boil)
- In a separate bowl, whisk egg yolks and sugar until pale and smooth
- Slowly add warm milk mixture to the eggs while whisking (tempering)
- Return mixture to the saucepan and cook on low heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens slightly
- Remove from heat and add vanilla extract and salt
- Let the mixture cool, then refrigerate for at least 2–4 hours


## Churning

- Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker
- Churn according to the machine instructions (usually 20–30 minutes)
- Transfer to a container and freeze for 2–3 hours before serving
